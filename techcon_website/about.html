History

Since its inception in 2012, TechCon has grown from a modest developer meetup into one of the most anticipated tech gatherings in Africa. What began as a one-day workshop hosted at a university hall has now transformed into a multi-day conference attracting tech leaders, founders, engineers, and students from across the continent. Over the years, TechCon has introduced new tracks in artificial intelligence, web development, cloud computing, and more, continually adapting to the fast-paced evolution of technology. Each edition builds on the last, empowering communities, launching startups, and spotlighting groundbreaking solutions made in Africa.

Mission

At TechCon, we believe in the power of technology to drive positive change. Our mission is to connect developers, innovators, and decision-makers to exchange ideas, collaborate on solutions, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. We aim to create an inclusive space where voices from all walks of life are heard, and where knowledge sharing fuels growth. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned professional, TechCon offers the platform to learn, share, and lead the change we want to see in the tech ecosystem.

Past Speakers

Over the past editions, TechCon has hosted a diverse lineup of trailblazing speakers whose insights have sparked inspiration and action. From Jane Mwangi, CTO of a Nairobi-based AI firm, who spoke on building ethical machine learning models, to Peter Otieno, a veteran software engineer from Safaricom, who shared his journey of building scalable APIs, the speaker roster reflects a commitment to real-world experience and thought leadership. We’ve also welcomed international voices, such as DevRel specialists from GitHub and Mozilla, who brought a global perspective to local challenges. Their stories continue to shape the narrative of Africas tech revolution.
